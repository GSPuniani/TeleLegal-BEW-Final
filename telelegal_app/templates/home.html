For Potential Clients

Simple

If you (Potential Client) are seeking legal help or advice, simply fill out the Request Form here. A qualified attorney will reach out to you at the email address that you provide in the form. If you and the attorney agree to work on the case together, then the attorney will set up video consultation sessions to work on your case with you.

Convenient

Potential Clients have access to attorneys across the entire State of California. All communication will be over text or video, so there is no need to physically meet at any legal office. Attorneys are generally quick to answer requests.

Affordable

Potential Clients do not pay anything up front. The emails or text chat messages exchanged with the attorney after submitting the Request Form are free of charge. The Potential Client and the attorney will make their own arrangements for reasonable prices during any video consultations. Many routine services, such as drafting a simple will or reviewing a standard contract, may be offered at a flat rate.

Benefits to Attorneys

With TeleLegal, attorneys of all experience levels have access to Potential Clients all over California. This is especially useful for newly instated attorneys who do not yet have an expansive network from which to establish their practice. However, more seasoned attorneys can also take advantage of TeleLegal's service to earn extra income by completing small cases or services on the side, branching out of their primary practice areas, or reaching clients who otherwise struggle with finding legal help.
